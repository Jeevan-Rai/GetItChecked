Plagiarism is a developing issue that is often characterised as literary theft and academic dishonesty in the literature; therefore, it must be avoided and adhere to ethical norms. Plagiarism has a major negative impact on education in a number of ways, including by impeding students' ability to acquire critical analysis and creative thinking skills and by eroding teacher-student relationships. Additionally, plagiarism that goes unnoticed might harm the academic institution's reputation and diminish the value of its degrees.
